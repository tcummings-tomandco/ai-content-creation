## Primary and named citations (inline in article)

### 1. DSIT AI Adoption Research
- **URL**: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/ai-adoption-research/ai-adoption-research
- **Publisher**: Department for Science, Innovation and Technology (DSIT), UK Government
- **Published**: 13 February 2026 (fieldwork 12 February – 2 May 2025; 3,500 UK private-sector business interviews, minimum 5 employees, plus 100 qualitative follow-ups)
- **Claims supported**:
  - Around 1 in 6 UK businesses (16%) currently use at least one AI technology.
  - Among AI adopters, 85% use natural language processing / text generation.
  - Among AI adopters, 7% use agentic AI ("likely due to its relative newness").
  - Around a third of businesses (32%) reported significant barriers implementing agentic AI, vs 18% for text generation.
- **Accessed**: 2026-07-01

### 2. MIT Sloan — "Agentic AI, explained"
- **URL**: https://mitsloan.mit.edu/ideas-made-to-matter/agentic-ai-explained
- **Publisher**: MIT Sloan School of Management
- **Published**: 18 February 2026
- **Claims supported**: Definition of agentic AI as "autonomous software systems that perceive, reason, and act in digital environments to achieve goals"; use of APIs as building blocks; Professor Kate Kellogg quote that these systems "can complete an entire workflow with multiple steps and execute actions."
- **Accessed**: 2026-07-01

### 3. Anthropic — "Building Effective Agents"
- **URL**: https://www.anthropic.com/research/building-effective-agents

## Layer 2 original calculation (Tom & Co analysis)

**Claim in article**: "Tom & Co analysis of the DSIT February 2026 figures puts generative or text-generation AI in the hands of roughly 13.6% of all UK businesses, while agentic AI reaches only about 1.1%. That is a roughly 12x difference in real-world reach today."

**Method (Recipe C — derivation from a single primary snapshot the source did not publish as a share of all firms):**
- DSIT: 16% of all UK businesses use at least one AI technology.
- DSIT: among AI adopters, 85% use text generation and 7% use agentic AI.
- Share of all UK businesses using text generation = 0.16 × 0.85 = 0.136 = 13.6%.
- Share of all UK businesses using agentic AI = 0.16 × 0.07 = 0.0112 ≈ 1.1%.
- Ratio among adopters = 85% / 7% ≈ 12.1x (also holds across all firms since the 16% base cancels).
- **Result**: agentic AI reaches roughly 1.1% of all UK businesses vs 13.6% for generative/text-generation AI, a roughly 12x gap.

**Source used**: DSIT AI Adoption Research (published 13 February 2026), figures above. Cited in the article as "Tom & Co analysis of the DSIT February 2026 figures."
